## Overview

Candesartan has evidence for reducing heart failure hospitalizations and is one of the ARBs approved for heart failure management.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Candesartan: approved for heart failure — reduces hospitalizations. Standard ARB monitoring applies.
