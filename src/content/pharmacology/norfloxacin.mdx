## Overview

Norfloxacin is an older fluoroquinolone with limited systemic distribution, primarily concentrated in the urinary tract. It is largely replaced by ciprofloxacin and levofloxacin in clinical practice.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Norfloxacin: limited to UTI/prostatitis use (poor systemic distribution). Same Black Box Warnings as all fluoroquinolones. Largely replaced by newer fluoroquinolones.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**Fluoroquinolones all share: tendon rupture, neuropathy, myasthenia worsening (Black Box Warnings)**
