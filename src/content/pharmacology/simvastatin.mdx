## Overview

Simvastatin is a potent statin but has significant drug interactions. The 80 mg dose was associated with high rates of myopathy and is rarely used. Take in the evening for maximal cholesterol synthesis inhibition.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Simvastatin 80 mg = HIGH MYOPATHY RISK. Avoid with CYP3A4 inhibitors. Max 20 mg with amlodipine. Take in EVENING (cholesterol synthesis peaks at night). Contraindicated in pregnancy.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Simvastatin = Take in the EVENING'** — liver makes cholesterol mostly at night; 80 mg dose is dangerous
