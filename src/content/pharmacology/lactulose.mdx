## Overview

Lactulose treats constipation and is uniquely used for hepatic encephalopathy — its acidification of the colon traps ammonia as ammonium ion (NH4+), reducing blood ammonia levels and improving mental status.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Lactulose for HEPATIC ENCEPHALOPATHY: goal = 2-3 soft stools/day (reduces blood ammonia). Onset 24-48 hours. Monitor electrolytes. Acidifies colon to trap ammonia as NH4+.
