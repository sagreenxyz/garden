## Overview

Atenolol is a cardioselective beta-1 blocker excreted renally, making dose adjustment necessary in renal impairment. Widely used for hypertension and angina.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Atenolol is renally cleared — reduce dose in renal failure. Hold if apical HR < 60. Do not abruptly discontinue.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'A-tenolol = A kidney drug'** — renal excretion means dose adjustment in CKD
