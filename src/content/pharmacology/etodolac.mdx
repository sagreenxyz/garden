## Overview

Etodolac preferentially inhibits COX-2 at therapeutic doses, providing anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects with some GI safety advantage. It is used for arthritis and acute pain.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Etodolac: preferential COX-2 inhibitor — some GI safety. Take with food. Standard NSAID monitoring: renal function, BP, GI symptoms.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**NSAIDs as a class: avoid in GI ulcers, renal failure, third trimester, post-CABG**
