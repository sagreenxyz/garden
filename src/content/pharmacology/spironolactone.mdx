## Overview

Spironolactone is a potassium-sparing diuretic that blocks aldosterone. It reduces mortality in systolic heart failure (RALES trial) and is used for ascites, hypertension, and primary hyperaldosteronism.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Spironolactone SAVES POTASSIUM — hyperkalemia risk. Avoid K+ supplements. Monitor K+ carefully, especially with ACE inhibitors/ARBs. Gynecomastia is a common side effect in men.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Spironolactone SPARES potassium'** — potassium-sparing, monitor for hyperkalemia and gynecomastia
