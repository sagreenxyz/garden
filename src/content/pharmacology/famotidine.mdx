## Overview

Famotidine is the preferred H2 blocker due to minimal drug interactions compared to cimetidine. It reduces gastric acid but is less potent than PPIs. IV doses can prolong QTc.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Famotidine: safest H2 blocker (fewest drug interactions). Dose reduce in renal impairment. IV high doses: QT prolongation — monitor ECG. Less effective than PPIs.
