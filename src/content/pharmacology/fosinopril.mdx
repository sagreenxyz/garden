## Overview

Fosinopril is unique among ACE inhibitors for its dual hepatic and renal elimination, making it useful in patients with renal impairment without requiring full dose adjustment.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Fosinopril = dual elimination (liver + kidney) — safer in renal impairment than other ACE inhibitors.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Fosinopril = Flexible elimination'** — dual hepatic/renal — safer in CKD
