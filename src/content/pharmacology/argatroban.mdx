## Overview

Argatroban is a direct thrombin inhibitor used for HIT. It is hepatically cleared, making bivalirudin preferred in hepatic failure. It affects INR measurement when transitioning to warfarin.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Argatroban for HIT — monitor aPTT. HEPATICALLY cleared — avoid/reduce in liver failure. Elevates INR when transitioning to warfarin (use chromogenic factor X assay to monitor warfarin).
