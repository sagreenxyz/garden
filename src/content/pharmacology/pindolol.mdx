## Overview

Pindolol has the strongest intrinsic sympathomimetic activity of all beta blockers, making it less likely to cause bradycardia at rest. It is used primarily for hypertension.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Pindolol has strong ISA — least bradycardia of beta blockers. Still contraindicated in asthma (non-selective).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Pindolol = Partial agonist'** — strong ISA, least bradycardia
