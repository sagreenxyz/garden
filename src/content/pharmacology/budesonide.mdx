## Overview

Budesonide has high first-pass hepatic metabolism, limiting systemic absorption from inhaled, nasal, and oral forms. This makes it one of the safer inhaled corticosteroids with minimal systemic effects at therapeutic doses.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Rinse mouth after budesonide INHALATION (thrush prevention). Not for acute attacks — maintenance only. Oral form (Entocort) has high first-pass = low systemic effects in Crohn disease.
