## Overview

Nadolol is a non-selective beta blocker with a long half-life allowing once-daily dosing. It is renally excreted, requiring dose adjustment in renal impairment.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Nadolol = once daily, renally cleared. Contraindicated in asthma (non-selective). Reduce dose in CKD.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Nadolol = long duration'** — once daily, renal clearance
