## Overview

Escitalopram is highly selective for serotonin reuptake with minimal drug interactions compared to other SSRIs, making it particularly well-tolerated. It carries a QT prolongation risk at higher doses.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Escitalopram: fewest drug interactions of SSRIs (minimal CYP450). QT prolongation concern. 14-day MAOI washout. Reduce dose in elderly and liver disease. Same suicidality Black Box Warning.
