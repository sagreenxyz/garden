## Overview

Oxycodone is commonly prescribed for moderate-severe pain. Extended-release forms are high-risk — crushing releases the full dose immediately, causing fatal overdose.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Extended-release oxycodone: NEVER crush (dose-dumping = fatal). If combined with acetaminophen (Percocet), monitor total APAP dose. Antidote = naloxone. Monitor respiratory rate.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'OxyContin whole only — never crush'** — dose-dumping from crushing extended-release = overdose
