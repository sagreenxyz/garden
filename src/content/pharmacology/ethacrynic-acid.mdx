## Overview

Ethacrynic acid is the only non-sulfonamide loop diuretic, making it the agent of choice in patients with sulfonamide allergies who require loop diuretic therapy. It has greater ototoxicity than furosemide.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Ethacrynic acid = only non-sulfonamide loop diuretic. Use in sulfonamide allergic patients. Higher ototoxicity risk — avoid with aminoglycosides. Monitor K+.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
