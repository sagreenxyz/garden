## Overview

Celecoxib is the only COX-2 selective inhibitor available in the US. Its GI safety advantage is offset by higher cardiovascular risk. Cross-reactivity with sulfonamide allergies must be considered.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Celecoxib: COX-2 selective = less GI risk, but HIGHER CV risk. Check sulfonamide allergy (structural relation). Avoid post-CABG. Still contraindicated in third trimester.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'COX-2 selective = better GI, worse heart'** — celecoxib has lower GI risk but higher cardiovascular risk
