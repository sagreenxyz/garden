## Overview

Moexipril is an ACE inhibitor prodrug used for hypertension. It should be administered on an empty stomach as food significantly reduces its absorption.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Moexipril absorption is reduced 40-50% by food — give on empty stomach. Standard ACE inhibitor monitoring: K+, renal function, angioedema.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Moexipril = empty stomach'** — food reduces absorption significantly
