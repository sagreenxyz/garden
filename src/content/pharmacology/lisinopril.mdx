## Overview

Lisinopril is one of the most commonly prescribed ACE inhibitors. It reduces both preload and afterload and slows progression of diabetic nephropathy.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Angioedema is a life-threatening emergency — stop ACE inhibitor IMMEDIATELY. ACE inhibitors cause dry cough due to bradykinin accumulation. CONTRAINDICATED in pregnancy (teratogen — Class D).
