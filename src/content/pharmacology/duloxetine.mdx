## Overview

Duloxetine has FDA approvals for multiple pain conditions including diabetic neuropathy, fibromyalgia, and chronic musculoskeletal pain, distinguishing it from SSRIs. Hepatotoxicity requires caution with alcohol use.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Duloxetine: FDA-approved for fibromyalgia, diabetic neuropathy, chronic pain (advantage over SSRIs). Hepatotoxicity — avoid substantial alcohol. 14-day MAOI washout. Monitor BP. Discontinuation syndrome.
