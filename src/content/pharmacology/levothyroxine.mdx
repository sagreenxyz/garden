## Overview

Levothyroxine is the standard treatment for hypothyroidism. It must be taken consistently in the morning on an empty stomach, separated from numerous medications that impair absorption.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Levothyroxine: take FASTING in the MORNING, 30-60 min before breakfast. Separate from calcium/iron/antacids by 4 hours. Monitor TSH. Narrow therapeutic index — avoid switching brands.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Synthroid = morning, fasting, separate from calcium'** — absorption greatly affected by food and other drugs
