## Overview

Rifampin is a rifamycin antibiotic used in multi-drug TB regimens. Its most significant property is potent CYP3A4 induction, dramatically reducing levels of many drugs including oral contraceptives and antiretrovirals.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Rifampin: POTENT CYP INDUCER — reduces OCP efficacy (backup contraception needed), warfarin (INR drops), HIV drugs, methadone. Orange discoloration of ALL body fluids (harmless but stains contacts). NEVER monotherapy for TB.
