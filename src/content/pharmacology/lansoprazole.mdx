## Overview

Lansoprazole is available as an orally disintegrating tablet (Prevacid SoluTab), making it useful for patients with difficulty swallowing or via NG tube.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Lansoprazole ODT dissolves on tongue without water — useful for swallowing difficulty. Take 30-60 min before meals. Standard PPI monitoring: Mg++, B12, C. diff.
