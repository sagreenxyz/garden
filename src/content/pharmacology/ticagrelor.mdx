## Overview

Ticagrelor is directly active (not a prodrug), providing faster and more consistent platelet inhibition than clopidogrel. The PLATO trial showed superiority over clopidogrel in ACS outcomes.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Ticagrelor: dyspnea in 14% (usually benign — reassure patient). Take with 81 mg aspirin ONLY (higher doses reduce efficacy). Twice daily. Direct action — not a prodrug. Hold 5 days pre-surgery.
