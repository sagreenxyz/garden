## Overview

Triamcinolone is commonly used for intra-articular injection of inflamed joints. Repeated joint injections can cause cartilage damage, so injections are limited to 3-4 per joint per year.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Triamcinolone intra-articular: limit to 3-4 injections per joint per year (cartilage damage). Monitor blood glucose after injection. Intranasal: spray away from septum.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Triamcinolone joint injections: max 3-4/year per joint'** — repeated injections damage cartilage
