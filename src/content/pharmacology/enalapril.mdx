## Overview

Enalapril is a prodrug ACE inhibitor available in both oral and IV (enalaprilat) forms. Its IV form is useful in hypertensive emergencies when oral therapy is not possible.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Enalapril is a PRODRUG — requires hepatic conversion. IV form = enalaprilat. Stop for angioedema or pregnancy.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Enalapril = Prodrug, has IV form'** — unique among common ACE inhibitors
