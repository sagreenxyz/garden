## Overview

Daptomycin is a unique cyclic lipopeptide antibiotic active against MRSA and VRE. Its critical limitation is inactivation by pulmonary surfactant — it must NEVER be used for pneumonia.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Daptomycin: NEVER use for PNEUMONIA (inactivated by surfactant). Monitor CK weekly (myopathy/rhabdomyolysis). Hold statins during therapy. MRSA bacteremia and endocarditis.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Daptomycin + lungs = NO'** — inactivated by pulmonary surfactant; monitor CK for myopathy
