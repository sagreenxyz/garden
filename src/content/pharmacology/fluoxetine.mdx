## Overview

Fluoxetine has the longest half-life of all SSRIs (4-16 days including active metabolite), requiring a 5-week washout before starting an MAOI. It is the SSRI with the lowest discontinuation syndrome risk.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Fluoxetine: LONGEST half-life SSRI. MAOI washout = 5 WEEKS after stopping (vs 2 weeks for others). CYP2D6 inhibitor. Less discontinuation syndrome. Same suicidality Black Box Warning.
