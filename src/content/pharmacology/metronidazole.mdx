## Overview

Metronidazole is the prototype nitroimidazole effective against anaerobes and protozoa. Its disulfiram-like reaction with alcohol requires strict avoidance of alcohol during and after treatment.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Metronidazole: AVOID ALCOHOL during treatment AND 3 days after (disulfiram reaction). Metallic taste is very common. Urine turns reddish-brown (harmless). Avoid in first trimester. Effective for C. diff, anaerobes, protozoa.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Metronidazole (Flagyl) + alcohol = disulfiram reaction'** — avoid all alcohol during treatment and 72 hours after
