## Overview

Fluconazole is the most widely used azole antifungal. Its single-dose convenience makes it preferred for vaginal candidiasis. As a CYP2C9/3A4 inhibitor, it significantly increases levels of many drugs, especially warfarin.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Fluconazole: CYP2C9 + CYP3A4 INHIBITOR — significantly increases warfarin (check INR), sulfonylureas, phenytoin. Single 150 mg dose treats vaginal candidiasis. Monitor LFTs.
