## Overview

Pitavastatin has the fewest drug interactions of all statins due to minimal CYP450 metabolism. Despite this, it is still contraindicated with cyclosporine.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Pitavastatin: fewest drug interactions (minimal CYP450). Still contraindicated with cyclosporine. Monitor for myopathy and LFTs.
