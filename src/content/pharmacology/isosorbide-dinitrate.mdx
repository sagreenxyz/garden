## Overview

Isosorbide dinitrate combined with hydralazine (BiDil) is FDA-approved specifically for Black patients with heart failure, reducing mortality when ACE inhibitors/ARBs are not tolerated.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Isosorbide dinitrate + hydralazine (BiDil) = FDA-approved for Black patients with HFrEF. Nitrate-free period required. Avoid with PDE-5 inhibitors. Headache common.
