## Overview

Bupropion is unique among antidepressants — no serotonin effects, no sexual dysfunction, and modest weight loss. Its main risk is dose-dependent seizure induction, especially in eating disorder patients.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Bupropion: SEIZURE RISK (dose-dependent) — contraindicated in eating disorders, seizure history, alcohol/benzo withdrawal. No sexual dysfunction (dopaminergic). No weight gain. No serotonin syndrome. Smoking cessation.
