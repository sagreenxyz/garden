## Overview

Tigecycline is a glycylcycline reserved for resistant infections. An FDA Black Box Warning notes increased all-cause mortality compared to other antibiotics — use only when alternatives are unavailable.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Tigecycline: FDA warning — INCREASED ALL-CAUSE MORTALITY vs. other antibiotics. Use only when alternatives not available. Nausea/vomiting very common. IV only.
