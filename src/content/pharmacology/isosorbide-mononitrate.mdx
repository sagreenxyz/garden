## Overview

Isosorbide mononitrate is a long-acting nitrate used for angina prophylaxis. A nitrate-free interval is essential to prevent tolerance. Extended-release once-daily in the morning is a common regimen.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Isosorbide mononitrate: schedule to maintain NITRATE-FREE PERIOD to prevent tolerance. Twice-daily: 7am and 2pm (not at night). Avoid PDE-5 inhibitors. Headache is expected early.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Nitrate-free period prevents tolerance'** — give in AM only or with 10-12 hour break
