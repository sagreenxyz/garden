## Overview

Dipyridamole is used in combination with aspirin (Aggrenox) for secondary stroke prevention and as a pharmacologic stressor in cardiac stress testing. IV dipyridamole vasodilation is reversed by aminophylline.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , IV dipyridamole stress test: antidote is AMINOPHYLLINE (reverses vasodilation/bronchospasm). Headache is common with oral use — reassure patient. Avoid caffeine before dipyridamole stress test.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Dipyridamole stress test antidote = Aminophylline'** — reverses the vasodilatory effects
