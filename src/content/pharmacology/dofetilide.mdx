## Overview

Dofetilide is indicated only for AF/flutter and requires mandatory in-hospital initiation with at least 3 days of continuous ECG monitoring. It is available only through a REMS program due to proarrhythmia risk.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Dofetilide: must START in hospital with 3-day ECG monitoring. Restricted distribution (REMS). Adjust for RENAL function. Stop if QTc > 500 ms. Torsades de pointes risk is dose-dependent.
