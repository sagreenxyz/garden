## Overview

Fondaparinux selectively inhibits factor Xa and does not cause HIT, making it an important alternative for HIT patients. However, there is NO antidote, and it accumulates in renal failure.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Fondaparinux: NO ANTIDOTE (use rFVIIa off-label if needed). Does NOT cause HIT — safe in HIT patients. Avoid in CrCl < 30. SQ abdomen only.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Fondaparinux = no HIT, no antidote'** — HIT-safe but no reversal agent
