## Overview

Amphotericin B is the most broad-spectrum antifungal, used for serious life-threatening fungal infections. Nephrotoxicity and infusion reactions ('Ampho-terrible') are major limitations. Lipid formulations significantly reduce nephrotoxicity.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Amphotericin B: NEPHROTOXIC — monitor BUN/creatinine daily. PREMEDICATE for infusion reactions (Tylenol, Benadryl). Supplement K+ and Mg++ aggressively. Lipid forms (AmBisome) = much less nephrotoxicity. Do NOT use conventional in D5W.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
