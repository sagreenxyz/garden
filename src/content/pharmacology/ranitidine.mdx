## Overview

Ranitidine was withdrawn from the US market in 2020 due to unacceptable levels of N-nitrosodimethylamine (NDMA), a probable human carcinogen. Famotidine is now the preferred H2 blocker.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Ranitidine (Zantac) WITHDRAWN due to NDMA contamination. Advise patients to use famotidine (Pepcid) instead. This is NCLEX-relevant — nurses must counsel patients about discontinued medications.
