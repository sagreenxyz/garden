## Overview

Erythromycin is the prototype macrolide with the highest GI side effect profile due to its motilin receptor agonism. At low doses, it is used as a prokinetic for gastroparesis. Eye drops are given at birth for ophthalmia neonatorum prophylaxis.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Erythromycin: highest GI side effects of macrolides (motilin receptor agonist). LOW DOSE = prokinetic for gastroparesis. Eye drops for neonatal ophthalmia prophylaxis. CYP3A4 inhibitor + QT risk.
