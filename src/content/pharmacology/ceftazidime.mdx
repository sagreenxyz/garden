## Overview

Ceftazidime has the best Pseudomonas coverage among 3rd-generation cephalosporins but has less gram-positive activity than ceftriaxone. It is used for cystic fibrosis pulmonary infections and Pseudomonas meningitis.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Ceftazidime: best Pseudomonas coverage of 3rd gen cephalosporins, but less gram-positive activity than ceftriaxone. Dose adjust in renal failure.
