## Overview

Fentanyl is a highly potent, lipophilic opioid 50-100x more potent than morphine. Transdermal patches are designed for opioid-tolerant patients with chronic pain. Heat exposure dramatically increases patch absorption.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Fentanyl patch: NEVER use in opioid-naive patients. NO HEAT on patch site (fever/heating pad = overdose). 72-hour patch change. Rapid IV = chest wall rigidity (wooden chest). Antidote = naloxone. Even used patches lethal to children.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Fentanyl patch + heat = danger'** — fever or heating pads accelerate absorption causing overdose
