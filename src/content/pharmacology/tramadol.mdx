## Overview

Tramadol has a dual mechanism — weak opioid plus serotonin-norepinephrine reuptake inhibition. It lowers the seizure threshold and can cause serotonin syndrome when combined with other serotonergic drugs.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Tramadol: risk of SEIZURES (especially with epilepsy or high doses). SEROTONIN SYNDROME risk with SSRIs/SNRIs/MAOIs. Partially reversed by naloxone. Do not use in children post-tonsillectomy.
