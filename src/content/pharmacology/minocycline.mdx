## Overview

Minocycline has excellent tissue penetration and unique vestibular side effects (dizziness, vertigo, tinnitus) not seen with other tetracyclines. Long-term use carries risk of drug-induced lupus.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Minocycline: VESTIBULAR TOXICITY (dizziness, vertigo) unique among tetracyclines. Drug-induced lupus with long-term use. Less photosensitivity than doxycycline. Avoid in children < 8 and pregnancy.
