## Overview

Carvedilol is a non-selective beta blocker with alpha-1 blocking activity. It is a cornerstone of heart failure therapy, reducing mortality in HFrEF when started low and titrated slowly.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Give carvedilol with food to reduce orthostatic hypotension. Start low, go slow in HF. Hold if HR < 60 or SBP < 90.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Coreg = Core Heart Drug'** — used for heart failure with alpha AND beta blockade
