## Overview

Captopril was the first ACE inhibitor developed and contains a sulfhydryl group responsible for unique side effects including taste disturbance and risk of agranulocytosis.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Captopril must be given on EMPTY STOMACH. Unique side effects: taste disturbance, agranulocytosis (check WBC). Short acting — multiple daily doses needed.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Captopril = empty stomach, tastes bad'** — give before meals, watch for taste changes
