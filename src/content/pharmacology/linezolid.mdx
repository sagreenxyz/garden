## Overview

Linezolid is active against MRSA and VRE with 100% oral bioavailability. It is a weak MAO inhibitor causing serotonin syndrome with serotonergic drugs and hypertensive crisis with tyramine-rich foods.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Linezolid: weak MAOI — avoid tyramine foods (hypertensive crisis) and serotonergic drugs (serotonin syndrome). Monitor CBC weekly (myelosuppression). 100% oral bioavailability (oral = IV efficacy).

## Drug Class Memory Aid
