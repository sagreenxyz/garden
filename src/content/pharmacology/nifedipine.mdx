## Overview

Nifedipine is a potent dihydropyridine CCB. Short-acting forms can cause dangerous reflex tachycardia and increased mortality in acute MI. Only extended-release forms are used for hypertension.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Short-acting nifedipine is DANGEROUS in hypertension and MI — causes reflex tachycardia. Only use EXTENDED-RELEASE for hypertension. Grapefruit juice greatly increases levels.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Short-acting nifedipine = NO for HTN'** — only extended-release is safe for hypertension
