## Overview

Aspirin irreversibly inhibits COX, providing lasting antiplatelet effects. At low doses (81-325 mg), it prevents cardiovascular events. At higher doses, anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects are achieved.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Aspirin in children with viral illness = REYE SYNDROME (hepatic failure, encephalopathy). Irreversible COX inhibition — effects last 7-10 days. Tinnitus = early toxicity sign. Antidote = sodium bicarbonate.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Aspirin in children + virus = Reye syndrome'** — NEVER give to children with chickenpox or flu
