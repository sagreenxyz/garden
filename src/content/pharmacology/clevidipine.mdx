## Overview

Clevidipine is an ultrashort-acting IV CCB formulated in a lipid emulsion. Its rapid onset and offset allow precise blood pressure control. Metabolized by plasma esterases — independent of hepatic/renal function.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Clevidipine in lipid emulsion — check for egg/soy allergy. Discard after 4 hours (infection risk). Must transition to oral therapy — risk of rebound hypertension.
