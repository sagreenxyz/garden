## Overview

Fludrocortisone is a potent synthetic mineralocorticoid used for the mineralocorticoid component of adrenal insufficiency treatment. Patients with primary adrenal insufficiency (Addison disease) need both hydrocortisone AND fludrocortisone.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Adrenal insufficiency replacement: hydrocortisone (glucocorticoid) + fludrocortisone (mineralocorticoid). Monitor BP (hypertension) and K+ (hypokalemia). Signs of too much: edema, hypertension, hypokalemia.
