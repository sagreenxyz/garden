## Overview

Warfarin is an oral anticoagulant with a narrow therapeutic index requiring regular INR monitoring. Hundreds of drug and food interactions make it challenging to manage.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ WARFARIN antidote = Vitamin K (slow) or FFP/PCC (fast). INR goal: 2-3 most indications, 2.5-3.5 for mechanical mitral valve. Hold warfarin 5 days before surgery. Drug interactions are extensive.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Warfarin = Watch the INR'** — narrow therapeutic index, many interactions, vitamin K antidote
