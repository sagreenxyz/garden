## Overview

Adenosine briefly terminates reentrant SVT by blocking AV node conduction. Its extremely short half-life (~10 seconds) requires rapid IV push. The brief asystole and symptoms are expected and harmless.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Adenosine: MUST give as rapid IV push + 20 mL NS flush (half-life < 10 sec). Expected side effects: chest tightness, dyspnea, feeling of doom (transient). Brief asystole expected. CONTRAINDICATED in asthma. Antidote: aminophylline.
