## Overview

Polyethylene glycol (PEG) is a safe, effective osmotic laxative with minimal systemic absorption. High-dose PEG with electrolytes (GoLYTELY) is used for colonoscopy bowel preparation.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ PEG (Miralax): onset 1-3 days, mix in any beverage. Bowel prep PEG (GoLYTELY): drink 4L as directed — stay near bathroom. Monitor electrolytes with high-dose bowel prep.
