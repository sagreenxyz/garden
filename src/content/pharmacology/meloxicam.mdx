## Overview

Meloxicam preferentially inhibits COX-2 at therapeutic doses, offering some GI safety advantage over traditional NSAIDs, though it is not as selective as celecoxib. Once-daily dosing aids adherence.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Meloxicam: preferentially COX-2 (not fully selective like celecoxib). Once daily. Monitor GI and renal function. Same pregnancy and post-CABG contraindications.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
