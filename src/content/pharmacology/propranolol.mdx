## Overview

Propranolol is the prototype non-selective beta blocker with diverse clinical uses including hypertension, arrhythmias, essential tremor, and migraine prophylaxis. Its beta-2 blockade makes it contraindicated in asthma.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Propranolol blocks beta-2 — CONTRAINDICATED in asthma/COPD. Non-selective: affects lungs. Check HR before every dose.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Propranolol = ALL beta receptors blocked'** — 1 AND 2. Avoid in asthma.
