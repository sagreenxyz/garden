## Overview

Nicardipine is available in IV form for hypertensive emergencies, providing controllable blood pressure reduction with easy titration. Change infusion site every 12 hours to prevent phlebitis.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ IV nicardipine for hypertensive emergencies — change IV site every 12 hrs (phlebitis risk). Titratable and continuous monitoring required.
