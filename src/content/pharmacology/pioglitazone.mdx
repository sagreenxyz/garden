## Overview

Pioglitazone improves insulin sensitivity through PPARγ activation. Its major contraindication is heart failure (all classes) due to fluid retention. Long-term use is associated with increased bladder cancer risk.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Pioglitazone: CONTRAINDICATED in heart failure (fluid retention causes worsening). Bladder cancer risk with long-term use. Fluid retention, edema, weight gain. Fracture risk in women.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
