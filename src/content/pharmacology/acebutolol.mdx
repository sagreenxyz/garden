## Overview

Acebutolol has intrinsic sympathomimetic activity (ISA), causing less bradycardia at rest. It carries a risk of drug-induced lupus with long-term use.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Acebutolol has ISA — less bradycardia. Watch for drug-induced lupus with long-term use (ANA positive, joint pain, rash).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Acebutolol = partial agonist'** — ISA means less bradycardia at rest
