## Overview

Torsemide has superior oral bioavailability (~80%) compared to furosemide (~50%), resulting in more predictable diuresis when transitioning from IV to oral therapy.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Torsemide 10 mg = furosemide 40 mg. Better oral bioavailability than furosemide — more predictable diuresis. Same K+ monitoring required.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
