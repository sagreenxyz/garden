## Overview

Cimetidine is the prototype H2 blocker but has the most drug interactions of any H2 blocker due to potent CYP450 inhibition. It is largely replaced by famotidine. Anti-androgenic effects can cause gynecomastia in males.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Cimetidine: MOST drug interactions of H2 blockers (CYP450 inhibitor). Causes GYNECOMASTIA in males (anti-androgenic). Causes confusion in elderly. Avoid when possible — use famotidine instead.
