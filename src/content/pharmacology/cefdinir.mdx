## Overview

Cefdinir is an oral 3rd-generation cephalosporin commonly used for respiratory and ear infections. A notable interaction with iron supplements causes red-colored stools, which is harmless but alarming.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Cefdinir + iron supplements = RED/MAROON STOOLS (harmless — reassure patient). 3rd gen: better gram-negative. Complete full course.
