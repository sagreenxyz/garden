## Overview

Procainamide is a Class IA antiarrhythmic with active metabolite NAPA. Long-term use causes drug-induced lupus. It is used IV in ACLS for WPW with rapid ventricular response.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Procainamide: drug-induced LUPUS (long-term) — ANA positive, arthralgias, rash. QT prolongation/Torsades risk. IV: give slowly (widened QRS/hypotension). Monitor NAPA levels in renal failure.
