## Overview

Hydrocodone is one of the most prescribed opioids in the US, often combined with acetaminophen. The combination requires monitoring total acetaminophen intake to prevent hepatotoxicity.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Hydrocodone + acetaminophen: monitor total APAP dose (max 4000 mg/day; max 2000 mg in liver disease). Antidote = naloxone. Monitor respiratory rate. Highest Schedule II (as of 2014 reclassification).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Vicodin/Norco = watch the TYLENOL'** — combination product, monitor total acetaminophen
