## Overview

Levofloxacin is a 'respiratory fluoroquinolone' with better gram-positive coverage than ciprofloxacin, making it preferred for community-acquired pneumonia. Same Black Box Warnings apply.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Levofloxacin = 'respiratory fluoroquinolone' — better gram-positive/pneumococcal coverage than cipro. Same Black Box Warnings (tendon, neuropathy, myasthenia). QT prolongation risk.
