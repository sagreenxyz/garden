## Overview

Doxycycline is the most widely used tetracycline with a long half-life allowing once or twice-daily dosing. It is first-line for Lyme disease, RMSF, and atypical pneumonia.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Doxycycline: AVOID in children < 8 (tooth discoloration) and pregnancy. Take with full glass of water and stay UPRIGHT 30 min (esophageal ulceration). PHOTOSENSITIVITY — use sunscreen. Chelates with calcium/antacids.
