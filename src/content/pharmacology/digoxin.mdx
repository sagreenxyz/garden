## Overview

Digoxin has a narrow therapeutic index and multiple toxicity triggers. Hypokalemia is the most common precipitant of toxicity. Early signs include GI symptoms and visual disturbances (yellow-green halos).

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , DIGOXIN TOXICITY: Early = N/V/anorexia + visual halos. Antidote = DIGIBIND (Digoxin Immune Fab). Hold if HR < 60. Hypokalemia triggers toxicity. Check drug levels (therapeutic 0.5-2 ng/mL).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Digoxin = Dig the narrow range'** — 0.5-2 ng/mL; yellow halos = toxicity; antidote = Digibind
