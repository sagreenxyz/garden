## Overview

Moxifloxacin has anaerobic activity unlike other fluoroquinolones and the longest QT prolongation risk. It lacks urinary concentration, making it ineffective for UTI treatment.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Moxifloxacin: most QT prolongation of fluoroquinolones. Anaerobic coverage — useful for intraabdominal infections. NOT for UTI (does not concentrate in urine). Same Black Box Warnings.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Moxifloxacin = anaerobic + QT risk'** — covers anaerobes; highest QT prolongation risk among fluoroquinolones
