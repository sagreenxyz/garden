## Overview

Psyllium is a bulk-forming laxative that must always be taken with a full glass of water. Without adequate water, it can cause esophageal or intestinal obstruction. It also lowers LDL cholesterol.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Psyllium: ALWAYS take with FULL glass of water — esophageal obstruction risk if swallowed dry. Drink immediately after mixing (thickens quickly). Separate from other medications by 1-2 hours (reduces absorption).

## Drug Class Memory Aid
