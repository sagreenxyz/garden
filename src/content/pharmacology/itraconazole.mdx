## Overview

Itraconazole has broader antifungal coverage than fluconazole, including endemic fungi (Histoplasma, Blastomyces) and some Aspergillus. Its negative inotropic effect makes it contraindicated in heart failure.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Itraconazole: CONTRAINDICATED in heart failure (negative inotrope). Capsule needs fatty meal + acidic beverage; solution on empty stomach (opposite!). CYP3A4 inhibitor.
