## Overview

Sertraline is one of the most widely prescribed SSRIs with a favorable side effect profile. Its Black Box Warning for increased suicidality in young adults requires close monitoring during the first months of therapy.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ SSRIs: Black Box Warning for SUICIDALITY in under-25s — monitor closely first 4 weeks. MAOI wait: 14 days before or after. Serotonin syndrome risk. Discontinuation syndrome if stopped abruptly. Takes 2-4 weeks to work.
