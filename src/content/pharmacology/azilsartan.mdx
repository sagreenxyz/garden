## Overview

Azilsartan is one of the newest and most potent ARBs, potentially achieving greater blood pressure reduction than other ARBs at standard doses.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Azilsartan may be more potent than other ARBs. Standard ARB monitoring: K+, renal function, avoid in pregnancy.
