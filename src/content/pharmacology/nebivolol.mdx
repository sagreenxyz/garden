## Overview

Nebivolol is a third-generation cardioselective beta blocker with the unique property of stimulating nitric oxide-mediated vasodilation, providing additional blood pressure lowering.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Nebivolol has vasodilatory effects via nitric oxide — may cause more hypotension than other beta blockers. Hold if HR < 60.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Nebivolol = NO vasodilation'** — unique NO-mediated vasodilation among beta blockers
