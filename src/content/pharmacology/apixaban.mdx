## Overview

Apixaban is a well-tolerated DOAC with evidence for lower bleeding risk compared to warfarin in AF (ARISTOTLE trial). Unlike rivaroxaban, it can be taken without food.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Apixaban antidote = andexanet alfa. Taken TWICE DAILY (unlike rivaroxaban once-daily). Can take without food. Hold 24-48 hrs before procedures. Do not stop abruptly in AFib.
