## Overview

Tenofovir (TDF) is an NRTI backbone of many HIV regimens and also treats hepatitis B. Abrupt discontinuation in HBV patients can cause severe hepatic flare. It is also used for HIV PrEP in combination with emtricitabine.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Tenofovir: discontinuing in HBV patients = HEPATIC FLARE (reactivation). Monitor renal function (Fanconi syndrome risk). Used for HIV PrEP with emtricitabine. Lactic acidosis risk (all NRTIs).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Tenofovir = HBV flare if stopped abruptly'** — never discontinue in HBV patients without monitoring; PrEP component
