## Overview

Naloxone is the pure opioid antagonist used to reverse opioid overdose. Its shorter half-life than many opioids means re-narcotization can occur, requiring careful monitoring and possible re-dosing or infusion.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Naloxone antidote for ALL opioids — monitor for RE-NARCOTIZATION (naloxone wears off before opioid). Give smallest effective dose in dependent patients (precipitates withdrawal). Nasal spray for community use (Narcan).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Narcan wears off before the opioid'** — monitor and re-dose; re-narcotization is a real risk
