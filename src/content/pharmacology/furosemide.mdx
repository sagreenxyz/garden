## Overview

Furosemide is the most commonly used loop diuretic, providing potent diuresis. It acts in the loop of Henle and is used for edema, hypertension, and acute pulmonary edema.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ IV furosemide: give NO FASTER than 4 mg/min to prevent ototoxicity. Monitor K+ — hypokalemia is common (potassium-WASTING). Daily weights: 1 kg = 1 L fluid. Give in AM to prevent nocturia.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'FUROSEMIDE flushes electrolytes'** — watch K+, Na+, Mg++, Ca++ (all go DOWN)
