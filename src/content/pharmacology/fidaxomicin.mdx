## Overview

Fidaxomicin is a macrolide antibiotic with minimal systemic absorption that is specifically designed for C. difficile colitis. It has a lower recurrence rate than oral vancomycin for C. diff treatment.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Fidaxomicin: used ONLY for C. diff (not absorbed systemically). Lower recurrence rate than vancomycin for C. diff. More expensive but preferred for recurrent cases.
