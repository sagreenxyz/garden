## Overview

Dapagliflozin is FDA-approved for both HFrEF and HFpEF (one of few drugs proven for heart failure with preserved ejection fraction). It also slows CKD progression independent of glucose lowering.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Dapagliflozin: approved for HFpEF (preserved EF) — unique. Also reduces CKD progression. Same SGLT-2 risks: euglycemic DKA, yeast infections, Fournier gangrene. Avoid in bladder cancer.
