## Overview

Oseltamivir (Tamiflu) is the most commonly used antiviral for influenza. It must be started within 48 hours of symptom onset to reduce illness duration. Neuropsychiatric events in children require monitoring.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Oseltamivir: must start within 48 HOURS of symptoms for benefit. Reduces flu duration ~1 day. Monitor children for neuropsychiatric behavior changes. Take with food to reduce nausea.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Tamiflu within 48 hours'** — minimal benefit if started after 48 hours; take with food
