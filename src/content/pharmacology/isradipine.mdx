## Overview

Isradipine is a vascular-selective dihydropyridine CCB used for hypertension. Available in both immediate and controlled-release formulations.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Standard dihydropyridine CCB monitoring: blood pressure, reflex tachycardia, peripheral edema. Avoid grapefruit juice.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**Dihydropyridine CCBs end in '-dipine'** — vascular selective, cause edema and flushing
