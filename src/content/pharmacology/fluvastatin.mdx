## Overview

Fluvastatin is metabolized by CYP2C9 rather than CYP3A4, giving it a different drug interaction profile. It is the least potent statin for LDL reduction.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Fluvastatin: CYP2C9 substrate (not 3A4). Least potent statin. Short half-life — take at bedtime. Fewer interactions with CYP3A4 inhibitors.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Fluvastatin = CYP2C9'** — different metabolism than most statins
