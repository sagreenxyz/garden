## Overview

Remdesivir was the first FDA-approved antiviral for COVID-19. Infusion-related bradycardia requires heart rate monitoring during administration. It is formulated with cyclodextrin, contraindicated in severe renal failure.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Remdesivir: monitor for BRADYCARDIA during infusion. Monitor LFTs. Avoid in GFR < 30 (cyclodextrin accumulation). 5-day inpatient or 3-day outpatient course for COVID-19.
