## Overview

Indomethacin is a potent NSAID with higher CNS side effects (frontal headaches) than other NSAIDs. It is uniquely used IV in premature infants to close a patent ductus arteriosus.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Indomethacin: HIGHEST CNS side effects of NSAIDs (frontal headache, confusion). Used for PDA closure in preterm infants. Avoid in elderly. Take with food.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
