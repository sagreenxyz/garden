## Overview

Hydromorphone (Dilaudid) is 5-7x more potent than morphine. Its highly concentrated injectable form (10 mg/mL) has caused fatal overdoses due to concentration errors. It is a HIGH ALERT medication.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Hydromorphone: HIGH ALERT — 5x more potent than morphine. Highly concentrated solution exists — double-check doses. Antidote = naloxone. Hold if RR < 12/min.
