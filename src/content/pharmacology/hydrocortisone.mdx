## Overview

Hydrocortisone is identical to endogenous cortisol and is the drug of choice for adrenal insufficiency replacement because it provides both glucocorticoid and mineralocorticoid activity.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Hydrocortisone = replacement for adrenal insufficiency. Stress dosing required during illness/surgery. IV Solu-Cortef for adrenal crisis — give IMMEDIATELY. Monitor K+ and Na+. Never stop abruptly.
