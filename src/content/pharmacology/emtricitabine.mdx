## Overview

Emtricitabine is a key NRTI component in HIV combination therapy and PrEP. Combined with tenofovir (Truvada), it is the first FDA-approved PrEP regimen. Skin hyperpigmentation of palms and soles is a benign, unique side effect.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Emtricitabine: lactic acidosis risk (all NRTIs). Skin hyperpigmentation of palms/soles — benign. HBV patients — do not stop abruptly (hepatic flare). Component of HIV PrEP (Truvada).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Emtricitabine + tenofovir = Truvada/PrEP'** — first FDA-approved HIV pre-exposure prophylaxis
