## Overview

Docusate sodium (Colace) is a stool softener that prevents constipation by allowing water to penetrate and soften stool. It is first-line for preventing constipation in opioid users and post-MI patients to prevent Valsalva straining.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Docusate: stool SOFTENER — no cramping, no stimulation. Takes 1-3 days. Do NOT use with mineral oil. First-line PREVENTION of constipation with opioids or post-MI (prevent Valsalva).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Colace prevents, not treats constipation'** — start at same time as opioids; takes 1-3 days
