## Overview

Cefepime is a 4th-generation cephalosporin covering both Pseudomonas and gram-positive organisms. Neurotoxicity (encephalopathy and seizures) can occur in renal failure if dose is not adjusted.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Cefepime (4th gen): covers Pseudomonas AND gram-positives. NEUROTOXICITY in renal failure — dose MUST be adjusted for CrCl. Monitor for encephalopathy, myoclonus, seizures.
