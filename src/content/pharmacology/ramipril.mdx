## Overview

Ramipril has strong evidence for reducing cardiovascular events in high-risk patients (HOPE trial). It is a first-line agent post-MI.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Ramipril reduces CV events in high-risk patients (HOPE trial). Watch for angioedema and hyperkalemia. Stop in pregnancy.
