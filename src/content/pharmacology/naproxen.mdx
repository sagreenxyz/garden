## Overview

Naproxen has a longer half-life than ibuprofen, allowing twice-daily dosing. It appears to have a slightly lower cardiovascular risk among NSAIDs but carries the same GI and renal risks.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Naproxen: twice-daily dosing due to longer half-life. Same GI, renal, and pregnancy risks as all NSAIDs. Take with food. Avoid in elderly and renal failure.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Naproxen = longer-lasting NSAID'** — twice daily dosing; same class risks apply
