## Overview

Atorvastatin is one of the most potent statins, widely used for cardiovascular risk reduction. The 4S, PROVE-IT, and TNT trials established its benefit in high-risk patients.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ STATINS: monitor for myopathy/rhabdomyolysis (muscle pain + dark urine = emergency). CONTRAINDICATED in pregnancy (teratogen). Atorvastatin: avoid grapefruit juice (CYP3A4). Check LFTs.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Statins = LDL DOWN, liver enzymes UP risk'** — myalgia common, rhabdomyolysis rare but serious
