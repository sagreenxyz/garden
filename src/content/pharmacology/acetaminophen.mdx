## Overview

Acetaminophen is the most commonly used analgesic/antipyretic worldwide. Overdose is the most common cause of acute liver failure in the US, and treatment with N-acetylcysteine must begin within 8 hours for maximum effectiveness.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Acetaminophen MAX dose: 4000 mg/day (3000 mg/day elderly/alcoholics). Overdose antidote = N-ACETYLCYSTEINE (NAC) — give ASAP, most effective within 8 hours. Most common cause of acute liver failure in US. Hidden in many OTC products.
