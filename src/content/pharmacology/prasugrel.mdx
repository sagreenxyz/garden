## Overview

Prasugrel is more potent than clopidogrel but carries higher bleeding risk. It is contraindicated in patients with prior stroke or TIA due to risk of intracranial hemorrhage (TRITON-TIMI 38).

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Prasugrel CONTRAINDICATED in prior stroke/TIA — fatal intracranial hemorrhage risk. Most potent P2Y12 inhibitor. Age > 75 or weight < 60 kg — use caution. Hold 7 days pre-surgery.
