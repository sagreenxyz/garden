## Overview

Telmisartan has the longest half-life of all ARBs (~24 hrs), ensuring 24-hour blood pressure control with once-daily dosing. It has additional PPARγ agonist activity.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Telmisartan: longest half-life ARB, primarily biliary elimination (avoid in hepatic impairment). Standard ARB monitoring.
