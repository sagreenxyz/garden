## Overview

Empagliflozin is an SGLT-2 inhibitor with cardiovascular mortality reduction and heart failure benefits beyond glucose control. Euglycemic DKA is a rare but critical risk — DKA can occur even with normal blood glucose.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Empagliflozin: SGLT-2 inhibitor — glucose excreted in urine. Euglycemic DKA risk (normal glucose + DKA — check KETONES). Fournier gangrene (rare emergency). Yeast infections common. Hold before surgery. EMPA-REG: reduces CV mortality.
