## Overview

Flecainide is contraindicated in structural heart disease after the CAST trial showed it increased mortality in post-MI patients. It is appropriate only for paroxysmal AF in patients with normal hearts.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Flecainide: ONLY for structurally NORMAL hearts — CAST trial showed increased mortality in post-MI patients. Monitor QRS width (widening = toxicity). Risk of proarrhythmia.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Flecainide = normal hearts only'** — CAST trial proved fatal in post-MI; avoid structural heart disease
