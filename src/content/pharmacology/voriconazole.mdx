## Overview

Voriconazole is first-line for invasive aspergillosis. Its unique visual disturbances (photopsia) are expected, transient, and harmless. Long-term photosensitivity increases risk of cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Voriconazole: VISUAL DISTURBANCES (photopsia) — expected, transient, warn patient. Drug of choice for ASPERGILLOSIS. Long-term: skin cancer risk from photosensitivity. CYP inhibitor — many interactions. Monitor LFTs and drug levels.
