## Overview

Cephalexin is the most commonly prescribed oral 1st-generation cephalosporin, with excellent coverage of gram-positive organisms including MSSA and streptococci.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Cephalexin (1st gen): best gram-positive coverage. Does NOT cover MRSA, Enterococcus, or most gram-negative. Cross-reactivity with penicillin allergy is rare (~1-2%) but avoid in anaphylaxis history. Complete full course.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'1st gen cephalosporins = gram-positive coverage'** — keflex for skin infections, does NOT cover MRSA
