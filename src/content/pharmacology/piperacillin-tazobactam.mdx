## Overview

Piperacillin-tazobactam (Pip-tazo) is a broad-spectrum IV antibiotic combining an extended-spectrum penicillin with a beta-lactamase inhibitor. It covers Pseudomonas and is used for severe hospital-acquired infections.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Pip-tazo (Zosyn): covers Pseudomonas and beta-lactamase producers. Monitor K+ (hypokalemia) and renal function. Extended infusion (4 hours) improves pharmacodynamic efficacy against resistant organisms.
