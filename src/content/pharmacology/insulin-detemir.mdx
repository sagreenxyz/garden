## Overview

Insulin detemir provides basal coverage but with a shorter duration than glargine, sometimes requiring twice-daily dosing. Like glargine, it must not be mixed with other insulins.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Insulin detemir: duration 18-24 hours — may need TWICE daily (unlike glargine). Do NOT mix with other insulins. Clear solution. Alternative basal insulin to glargine.
