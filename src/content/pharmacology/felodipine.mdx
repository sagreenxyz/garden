## Overview

Felodipine is a vascular-selective dihydropyridine CCB used for hypertension. Like nifedipine, it is significantly affected by grapefruit juice.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Felodipine + grapefruit juice = greatly increased drug levels and toxicity. Highly vascular selective — minimal cardiac effects.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Dihydropyridines + grapefruit = dangerous'** — felodipine especially affected
