## Overview

Olmesartan is a potent ARB with a unique risk of sprue-like enteropathy — severe, chronic diarrhea with intestinal villous atrophy that reverses on drug discontinuation.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Olmesartan unique risk: SPRUE-LIKE ENTEROPATHY — severe chronic diarrhea, weight loss, villous atrophy. Discontinue if this occurs.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Olmesartan = bowel problems'** — unique risk of sprue-like enteropathy with chronic diarrhea
