## Overview

Nisoldipine is a highly vascular selective dihydropyridine CCB used for hypertension. High-fat meals significantly increase its absorption.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Nisoldipine: avoid grapefruit juice AND high-fat meals (both increase absorption). Once-daily extended-release.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Nisoldipine = food effect'** — high fat meals increase absorption
