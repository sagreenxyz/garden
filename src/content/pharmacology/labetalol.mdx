## Overview

Labetalol combines alpha-1 and beta blockade, making it useful in hypertensive emergencies and pregnancy-induced hypertension. IV administration requires the patient to remain supine.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Keep patient SUPINE during and 3 hours after IV labetalol. Drug of choice for hypertension in pregnancy. Monitor BP continuously during IV.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Labetalol = Lay flat!'** — patient must be supine during IV administration
