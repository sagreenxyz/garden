## Overview

Clopidogrel is a prodrug requiring CYP2C19 activation. Poor metabolizers (up to 30% of patients) have reduced efficacy. Abrupt discontinuation after coronary stenting can cause life-threatening stent thrombosis.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Clopidogrel is a PRODRUG — needs CYP2C19 (less effective in poor metabolizers). Do NOT stop after stent — stent thrombosis risk. Omeprazole reduces efficacy. Hold 5-7 days pre-surgery.
