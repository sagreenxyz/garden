## Overview

Pantoprazole is the only PPI available both orally and IV, making it preferred in NPO hospitalized patients. IV infusion should be given over at least 15 minutes.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Pantoprazole: only PPI with IV form for hospitalized NPO patients. Give IV over 15 minutes. Standard PPI monitoring: Mg++, B12, C. diff risk. Avoid with atazanavir.
