## Overview

Insulin aspart (NovoLog) is a rapid-acting insulin analog equivalent to insulin lispro. Fiasp is an ultra-fast formulation of aspart. Both are used for mealtime insulin coverage.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Insulin aspart (NovoLog): give 5-10 min before meals. Fiasp (faster aspart): give 0-20 min after meal start. Both are rapid-acting — interchangeable with lispro. Can use IV in DKA.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'NovoLog/Fiasp = rapid-acting mealtime insulin'** — onset minutes; give just before eating
