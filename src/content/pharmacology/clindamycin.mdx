## Overview

Clindamycin has excellent coverage of gram-positive and anaerobic organisms. It is historically the antibiotic most associated with C. difficile colitis, requiring careful monitoring for diarrhea.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Clindamycin: HIGH C. DIFF RISK — classic precipitant of C. diff colitis. Report ANY diarrhea during or after treatment. Good for MRSA skin infections and anaerobes. Topical/vaginal = minimal systemic effects.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Clindamycin = C. diff risk'** — most classically associated with C. difficile colitis; monitor all diarrhea
