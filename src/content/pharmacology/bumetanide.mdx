## Overview

Bumetanide is a potent loop diuretic 40 times more potent than furosemide by weight. It may be used when furosemide is ineffective or in patients with furosemide allergy (sulfonamide consideration).

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Bumetanide dose equivalence: 1 mg = 40 mg furosemide. Same mechanism and monitoring as furosemide. Monitor K+, daily weights, urine output.
