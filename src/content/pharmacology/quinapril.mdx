## Overview

Quinapril is a high-affinity ACE inhibitor with significant tissue penetration. It is used for hypertension and heart failure.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Standard ACE inhibitor monitoring: potassium, renal function, angioedema. Avoid in pregnancy.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**ACE inhibitors all end in '-pril'** — class effect: cough, K+ increase, angioedema risk
