## Overview

Perindopril has clinical evidence for reducing cardiovascular events in patients with stable coronary artery disease, similar to ramipril.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Perindopril reduces CV events in stable CAD. Monitor for angioedema, cough, hyperkalemia. Avoid in pregnancy.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**ACE inhibitors for CAD**: ramipril (HOPE trial) and perindopril (EUROPA trial) both proven
