## Overview

Semaglutide is the most potent GLP-1 agonist for weight reduction and is available as both weekly injection (Ozempic) and oral tablet (Rybelsus). Wegovy (higher dose) is approved for obesity management.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Semaglutide: Rybelsus (oral) — take on EMPTY STOMACH with small sip of water, wait 30 min. Most potent GLP-1 for weight loss. Same class risks: medullary thyroid cancer, pancreatitis. Ozempic/Wegovy: once weekly SQ.
