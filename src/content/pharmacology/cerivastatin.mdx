## Overview

Cerivastatin was withdrawn from the market in 2001 due to an unacceptably high rate of fatal rhabdomyolysis, particularly when combined with gemfibrozil. It serves as an important historical lesson in statin safety.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Cerivastatin was WITHDRAWN due to fatal rhabdomyolysis. NCLEX context: know it was withdrawn from market. Reinforces why monitoring for muscle symptoms in ALL statin patients is critical.
