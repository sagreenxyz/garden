## Overview

Amiodarone is the most effective antiarrhythmic but has numerous serious toxicities due to its very long half-life and iodine content. It is the drug of choice in ACLS for refractory VF/pulseless VT.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Amiodarone toxicities (PALE): Pulmonary fibrosis, Abnormal thyroid, Liver damage, Eye problems (corneal deposits, optic neuropathy). Half-life = 40-55 days. In ACLS — first-line for refractory VF/VT.
