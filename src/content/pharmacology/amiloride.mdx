## Overview

Amiloride is a potassium-sparing diuretic that blocks sodium channels in the collecting duct independently of aldosterone. Often combined with thiazide diuretics to prevent hypokalemia.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Amiloride SPARES K+ — hyperkalemia risk. Avoid K+ supplements. Monitor K+ and renal function.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Potassium-sparing diuretics SAVE K+'** — amiloride, triamterene, spironolactone all increase K+
