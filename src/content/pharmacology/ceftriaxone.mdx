## Overview

Ceftriaxone is the most widely used IV cephalosporin with once-daily dosing, biliary elimination, and excellent CNS penetration. It is the drug of choice for bacterial meningitis and gonorrhea.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Ceftriaxone: CONTRAINDICATED in hyperbilirubinemic neonates (kernicterus) and with IV calcium in neonates (precipitation). Drug of choice for meningitis. Once-daily dosing. Biliary clearance — no renal dose adjustment.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Ceftriaxone + hyperbilirubinemic neonate = kernicterus'** — contraindicated; once-daily dosing
