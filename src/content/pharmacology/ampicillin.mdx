## Overview

Ampicillin is unique among beta-lactams as the drug of choice for Listeria meningitis, which is resistant to cephalosporins. This is a key NCLEX fact. Like amoxicillin, it causes rash in mononucleosis.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Ampicillin = drug of choice for LISTERIA meningitis (cephalosporins do NOT cover Listeria). Also covers enterococcus. Do NOT give in mononucleosis (rash). High diarrhea rate.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Ampicillin = covers Listeria'** — cephalosporins do NOT; key for neonatal and immunocompromised meningitis
