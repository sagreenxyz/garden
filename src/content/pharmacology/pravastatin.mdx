## Overview

Pravastatin is a hydrophilic statin with minimal CYP450 metabolism, resulting in fewer drug interactions and lower muscle toxicity. It is commonly used in transplant patients on cyclosporine.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Pravastatin: water-soluble, fewer CYP interactions — safer with cyclosporine in transplant patients. Less myopathy than lipophilic statins. Still contraindicated in pregnancy.
