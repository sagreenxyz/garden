## Overview

Nizatidine has a safety profile similar to famotidine with minimal drug interactions. It is available OTC for mild heartburn and by prescription for GERD and peptic ulcer disease.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Nizatidine: similar to famotidine, few drug interactions. Dose reduce in renal impairment. Less commonly used than famotidine.
