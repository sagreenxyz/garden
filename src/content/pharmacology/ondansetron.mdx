## Overview

Ondansetron is the most widely used antiemetic, with no extrapyramidal side effects unlike metoclopramide or promethazine. It carries a risk of QT prolongation with IV administration.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Ondansetron: NO extrapyramidal side effects (5-HT3 selective, no dopamine blockade). QT prolongation risk with IV — give slowly, monitor QTc. Max single IV dose reduced from 32 mg (arrhythmia risk). Safe in pregnancy.
