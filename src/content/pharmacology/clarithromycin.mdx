## Overview

Clarithromycin is a potent CYP3A4 inhibitor with extensive drug interactions. It is a key component of H. pylori eradication regimens. QT prolongation is a significant concern.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Clarithromycin: potent CYP3A4 INHIBITOR — increases levels of many drugs (statins = rhabdomyolysis risk). QT prolongation. H. pylori component. Metallic taste common.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Clarithromycin = CYP3A4 inhibitor + metallic taste'** — extensive drug interactions; rhabdomyolysis with statins
