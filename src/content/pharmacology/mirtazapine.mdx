## Overview

Mirtazapine is unique in causing significant sedation and weight gain due to antihistamine effects. This makes it beneficial in underweight, insomnia-associated depression. Unusually, lower doses are MORE sedating (antihistamine predominates).

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Mirtazapine: MORE SEDATING at LOW doses (7.5 mg) than high doses (antihistamine). Causes weight GAIN and appetite increase — useful in underweight depressed patients. No sexual dysfunction. Monitor CBC (agranulocytosis rare).
