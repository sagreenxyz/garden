## Overview

Sodium nitroprusside is the most potent IV vasodilator, producing immediate blood pressure reduction. It is metabolized to cyanide, making toxicity monitoring critical with prolonged use or high doses.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Nitroprusside: PROTECT FROM LIGHT (blue/green = discard). Monitor for cyanide toxicity (> 72h or high doses) — treat with hydroxocobalamin or sodium thiosulfate. Very potent — requires continuous monitoring.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Nitroprusside = wrap in foil + cyanide risk'** — light-sensitive, cyanide toxicity after prolonged use
