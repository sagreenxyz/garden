## Overview

Rabeprazole has fewer CYP2C19 interactions than omeprazole, potentially making it a better choice when used with clopidogrel, as it may less significantly reduce its antiplatelet efficacy.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Rabeprazole: fewer CYP2C19 drug interactions — may be safer with clopidogrel than omeprazole. Standard PPI monitoring and pre-meal dosing applies.
