## Overview

Amoxicillin is the most commonly prescribed oral antibiotic and the preferred aminopenicillin for many infections. It is notably contraindicated in infectious mononucleosis where it causes a characteristic widespread rash.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Amoxicillin in mononucleosis = widespread MACULOPAPULAR RASH. Check penicillin allergy (cross-react with cephalosporins ~1-2%). Complete full course. Report diarrhea (C. diff risk).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Amoxicillin + mono = rash'** — maculopapular rash in >90% of mono patients given amoxicillin
