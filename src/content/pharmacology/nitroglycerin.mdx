## Overview

Nitroglycerin is the prototype nitrate producing nitric oxide-mediated vasodilation. Used for acute angina, heart failure, and hypertensive emergencies. SL tablets are first-line for acute angina attacks.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Nitroglycerin + PDE-5 inhibitors (Viagra/Cialis) = FATAL HYPOTENSION — contraindicated. SL dose: sit down, place under tongue, max 3 doses Q5 min, call 911 if no relief after 1st. IV: use glass container and non-PVC tubing (drug absorbs into plastic). Headache is expected.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Nitro + Viagra = NO!'** — fatal hypotension; remove patch at night to prevent tolerance
