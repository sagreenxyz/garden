## Overview

Sotalol combines non-selective beta blockade with Class III antiarrhythmic properties (potassium channel blockade), prolonging the QT interval and increasing risk of torsades de pointes.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Sotalol PROLONGS QT — check baseline QTc, correct K+ and Mg++ before starting, hospitalize for first 3 days of therapy. AVOID in hypokalemia.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Sotalol = QT prolonger'** — must hospitalize first 3 days; check electrolytes
