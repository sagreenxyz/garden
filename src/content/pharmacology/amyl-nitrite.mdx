## Overview

Amyl nitrite is an inhaled nitrate used as the first step in cyanide poisoning treatment. It rapidly forms methemoglobin, which binds cyanide and removes it from cytochrome oxidase.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Cyanide antidote kit: Step 1 = Amyl nitrite (inhaled). Step 2 = Sodium nitrite (IV). Step 3 = Sodium thiosulfate (IV). Hydroxocobalamin is an alternative modern antidote.
