## Overview

Venlafaxine is the prototype SNRI with dose-dependent norepinephrine activity. At higher doses, it can cause hypertension. It has a significant discontinuation syndrome if stopped abruptly.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Venlafaxine: dose-dependent HYPERTENSION (monitor BP). Significant discontinuation syndrome — taper gradually. 14-day MAOI washout. Takes 2-4 weeks to work. Serotonin syndrome risk.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
