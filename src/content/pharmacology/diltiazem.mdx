## Overview

Diltiazem is a non-dihydropyridine CCB with both cardiac and vascular effects. It slows AV conduction making it useful for rate control in atrial fibrillation. IV diltiazem is used for acute rate control.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Never give IV diltiazem WITH IV beta blockers — risk of complete heart block/asystole. Check HR before each dose. PR interval prolongation is expected. Negative inotrope — avoid in decompensated HF.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Diltiazem = Dual action'** — cardiac AND vascular; rate control + BP lowering
