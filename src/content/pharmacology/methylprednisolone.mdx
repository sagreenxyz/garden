## Overview

Methylprednisolone is available in oral and IV forms. IV Solu-Medrol is used for acute inflammatory conditions including COPD exacerbations and severe allergic reactions. High-dose use after acute spinal cord injury has been controversial.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , IV methylprednisolone: give HIGH-DOSE slowly over 30+ minutes. Monitor blood glucose carefully — hyperglycemia is expected. Taper oral form — do not stop abruptly. Immunosuppressive — monitor for infection.
