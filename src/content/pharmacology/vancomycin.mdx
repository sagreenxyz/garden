## Overview

Vancomycin is the drug of choice for MRSA and serious gram-positive infections in penicillin-allergic patients. Red Man Syndrome is a common infusion-related reaction prevented by slow infusion. Nephrotoxicity requires monitoring.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Vancomycin: RED MAN SYNDROME from rapid infusion — slow down, give diphenhydramine (NOT a true allergy). Monitor TROUGH levels and renal function. DRUG OF CHOICE for MRSA. Oral form for C. diff (not absorbed).
