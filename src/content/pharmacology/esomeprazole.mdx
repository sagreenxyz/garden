## Overview

Esomeprazole is the S-enantiomer of omeprazole with somewhat more consistent pharmacokinetics. It is marketed as providing better acid control than omeprazole, though clinical differences are modest.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , Esomeprazole = S-isomer of omeprazole ('the purple pill'). Standard PPI: take 30-60 min before meals. Same long-term risks as all PPIs. Can dissolve granules in water.
