## Overview

Rosuvastatin is highly potent with fewer drug-drug interactions than atorvastatin or simvastatin (not metabolized by CYP3A4). Asian patients require lower doses due to higher plasma concentrations.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Rosuvastatin: NOT metabolized by CYP3A4 — fewer drug interactions. No grapefruit issue. Reduce dose in Asian patients. Monitor muscle pain and LFTs.
