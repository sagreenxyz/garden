## Overview

Metolazone retains diuretic effectiveness even at low GFR (unlike standard thiazides). Combined with loop diuretics, it produces synergistic, powerful diuresis useful in refractory heart failure edema.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Metolazone + furosemide = SYNERGISTIC diuresis. Give metolazone 30 minutes BEFORE furosemide. Monitor K+ very closely — risk of severe hypokalemia. Works in renal failure (unlike HCTZ).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Metolazone = Super thiazide'** — works in renal failure, give 30 min before loop for synergy
