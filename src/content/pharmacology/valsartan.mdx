## Overview

Valsartan is used for hypertension, heart failure, and post-MI LV dysfunction. It is also available in combination with sacubitril (Entresto) for heart failure.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Valsartan + sacubitril (Entresto) = ARNI — reduces HF hospitalizations and mortality. Monitor K+ and BP.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Valsartan in Entresto'** — combined with sacubitril (neprilysin inhibitor) for advanced HF
