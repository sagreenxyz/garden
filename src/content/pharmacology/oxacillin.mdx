## Overview

Oxacillin is the IV antistaphylococcal penicillin preferred for MSSA bacteremia and endocarditis. It is superior to vancomycin for MSSA but completely ineffective against MRSA.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Oxacillin: drug of choice for MSSA endocarditis/bacteremia (better than vancomycin for MSSA). Does NOT cover MRSA. Monitor LFTs (hepatotoxicity). Check sensitivity results before use.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'MSSA = oxacillin/nafcillin; MRSA = vancomycin'** — always confirm sensitivity before using
