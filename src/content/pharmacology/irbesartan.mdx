## Overview

Irbesartan is directly active (not a prodrug) and is eliminated via biliary/fecal route, making it suitable without dose adjustment in renal impairment.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Irbesartan is directly active (not prodrug) with non-renal elimination. Good choice in diabetic nephropathy. Standard ARB monitoring applies.
