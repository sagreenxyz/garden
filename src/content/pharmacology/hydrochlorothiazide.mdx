## Overview

Hydrochlorothiazide (HCTZ) is the most commonly used thiazide diuretic for hypertension. Unlike loop diuretics, thiazides retain calcium — useful in osteoporosis and calcium stone prevention.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ HCTZ retains calcium (opposite of loops). Causes hyperglycemia, hyperuricemia (gout), hypokalemia. Ineffective when GFR < 30 mL/min — switch to loop diuretic.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Thiazides = CALCIUM stays, K+ goes'** — hypercalcemia, hypokalemia, hyperuricemia, hyperglycemia
