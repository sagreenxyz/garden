## Overview

Glyburide is a longer-acting sulfonylurea with higher hypoglycemia risk than glipizide due to active metabolites. It is particularly dangerous in elderly patients and those with renal impairment.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Glyburide: HIGHER hypoglycemia risk than glipizide (longer-acting, active metabolites). AVOID in elderly and renal failure. Glipizide is the PREFERRED sulfonylurea in elderly patients. Alcohol interaction.
