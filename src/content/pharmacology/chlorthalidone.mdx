## Overview

Chlorthalidone is a thiazide-like diuretic with a longer half-life than HCTZ (~60 hrs), allowing once-daily or alternate-day dosing. Some studies suggest superior cardiovascular outcomes compared to HCTZ.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Chlorthalidone: longer half-life than HCTZ, possibly better CV outcomes (ALLHAT trial preferred it). Same monitoring as thiazides: K+, Na+, glucose, uric acid.
