## Overview

Lovastatin was the first FDA-approved statin. It is a prodrug that must be taken with food (evening meal) to maximize absorption. Multiple CYP3A4 drug interactions.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Lovastatin: take with EVENING MEAL (food improves absorption). CYP3A4 substrate — avoid grapefruit. First statin approved. Contraindicated in pregnancy.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Lovastatin with food at night'** — needs food for absorption, take at evening meal
