## Overview

Bisoprolol is one of three beta blockers (with metoprolol succinate and carvedilol) proven to reduce mortality in heart failure with reduced ejection fraction.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Bisoprolol, metoprolol succinate, and carvedilol are the only beta blockers proven to reduce mortality in HFrEF. Hold if HR < 60.
