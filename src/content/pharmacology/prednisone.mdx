## Overview

Prednisone is the prototype oral glucocorticoid. Long-term use causes numerous metabolic side effects including hyperglycemia, osteoporosis, Cushing syndrome, and adrenal suppression. Tapering is essential to prevent adrenal crisis.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ NEVER stop prednisone abruptly — adrenal crisis risk. Long-term: monitor blood glucose, blood pressure, bone density. Mnemonic: SLUDGE side effects of steroids (Sugar up, Lose K+, Ulcers, Dilated pupils, Growth suppression, Edema).

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Taper, don't stop steroids'** — abrupt discontinuation causes adrenal crisis; give in morning with food
