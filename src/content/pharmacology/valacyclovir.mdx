## Overview

Valacyclovir is a prodrug of acyclovir with 3-5x better oral bioavailability. It is the preferred oral herpes antiviral. High doses in severely immunocompromised patients can cause TTP/HUS.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Valacyclovir: prodrug of acyclovir — better oral bioavailability (preferred over oral acyclovir). HIGH DOSE in immunocompromised = TTP/HUS risk. Dose adjust in renal failure. Suppresses but does NOT cure herpes.
