## Overview

Dabigatran directly inhibits thrombin and has a specific antidote (idarucizumab). It has the highest GI side effect profile of DOACs and must be stored in original packaging due to moisture sensitivity.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Dabigatran antidote = IDARUCIZUMAB (Praxbind). DO NOT crush, do NOT put in pill organizer (absorbs moisture). Highest GI side effects of DOACs. Renally eliminated — avoid in severe renal failure.
