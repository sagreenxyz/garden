## Overview

Bivalirudin is a direct thrombin inhibitor with a short half-life and predominantly renal clearance. It is used during PCI and is an alternative to argatroban in HIT patients with liver failure.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Bivalirudin: renally cleared (use argatroban in liver failure). Short half-life — reversals happens quickly upon stopping. Monitor ACT during PCI. Safe in HIT.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
