## Overview

Trandolapril is a long-acting ACE inhibitor with evidence for use post-MI and in left ventricular dysfunction. Its once-daily dosing improves adherence.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Post-MI: ACE inhibitors (like trandolapril, lisinopril) reduce mortality. Monitor K+, creatinine, angioedema.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**Post-MI ACE inhibitors** reduce mortality — start within 24 hours if no contraindications
