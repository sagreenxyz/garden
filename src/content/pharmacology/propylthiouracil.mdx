## Overview

PTU is reserved for first-trimester pregnancy and thyroid storm (blocks T4→T3 conversion). Its Black Box Warning for hepatotoxicity (including liver failure) makes methimazole preferred after the first trimester.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ PTU: Black Box Warning for HEPATOTOXICITY (liver failure). Preferred in FIRST TRIMESTER (methimazole teratogenic) and THYROID STORM (blocks T4→T3 conversion — faster). Switch to methimazole after 1st trimester.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'PTU = first trimester + thyroid storm + liver risk'** — hepatotoxic; prefer methimazole except in 1st trimester
