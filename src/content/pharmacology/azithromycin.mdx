## Overview

Azithromycin (Z-pack) has a unique pharmacokinetic profile with tissue concentrations persisting 5-7 days after the last dose. It carries a QT prolongation risk — the FDA issued a cardiac safety alert.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Azithromycin: QT prolongation risk — FDA cardiac safety warning. Tissue levels persist 5-7 days after last dose. Single 1g dose for chlamydia. IV: give over 60 minutes (NOT rapid).
