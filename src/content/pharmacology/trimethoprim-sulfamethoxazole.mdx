## Overview

TMP-SMX (Bactrim) is a combination sulfonamide antibiotic and the drug of choice for community-acquired MRSA skin infections and PCP prophylaxis in HIV. SJS is a life-threatening risk.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ TMP-SMX: drug of choice for MRSA skin infections and PCP prophylaxis in HIV. SJS risk — stop immediately for rash/blistering. Causes HYPERKALEMIA (like K+-sparing diuretic). Avoid in sulfonamide allergy and third trimester.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Bactrim = MRSA + PCP coverage'** — SJS risk with rash; hyperkalemia from K+ blockade
