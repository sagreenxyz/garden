## Overview

Codeine is a prodrug requiring CYP2D6 conversion to morphine. FDA has restricted its use in children due to fatal respiratory depression in ultra-rapid metabolizers, especially post-tonsillectomy.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Codeine: BLACK BOX WARNING — do NOT use in children < 12 for ANY reason; avoid < 18 post-tonsillectomy (fatal respiratory depression in ultra-rapid metabolizers). Not safe in breastfeeding.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Codeine = NO in children after tonsillectomy'** — Black Box Warning; ultra-rapid metabolizers = morphine overdose
