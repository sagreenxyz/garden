## Overview

Rivaroxaban is a DOAC that directly inhibits factor Xa without requiring routine INR monitoring. For AF doses (15/20 mg), it must be taken with food to ensure adequate absorption.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Rivaroxaban: take 15/20 mg WITH FOOD. Antidote = andexanet alfa. No routine INR monitoring. Avoid in severe renal failure. Do not stop abruptly in AFib — stroke risk.
