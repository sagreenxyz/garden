## Overview

Paroxetine has the highest discontinuation syndrome risk of all SSRIs due to its short half-life. It also has the most anticholinergic and sedating effects and is associated with cardiac defects in first-trimester pregnancy.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Paroxetine: HIGHEST discontinuation syndrome (short half-life) — taper SLOWLY. Most sedating and anticholinergic SSRI. Most weight gain. Avoid in first trimester (cardiac defects). CYP2D6 inhibitor.
