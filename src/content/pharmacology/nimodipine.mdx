## Overview

Nimodipine prevents cerebral vasospasm after subarachnoid hemorrhage by selectively dilating cerebral arteries. It must ONLY be given orally — IV administration has caused fatal cardiac arrest.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ NIMODIPINE MUST ONLY BE GIVEN ORALLY — do NOT give IV (fatal cardiac arrests have occurred). Extract from capsule if patient cannot swallow. Q4h x 21 days for SAH.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
