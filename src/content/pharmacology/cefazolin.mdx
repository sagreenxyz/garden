## Overview

Cefazolin is the first-line drug for surgical prophylaxis and MSSA infections. It must be given 60 minutes before incision for perioperative prophylaxis. It does not cover MRSA.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Cefazolin: drug of choice for SURGICAL PROPHYLAXIS — give 60 minutes before incision. Does NOT cover MRSA. 1st gen: gram-positive best coverage. Give IV, not effective orally.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Cefazolin = surgical prophylaxis'** — give 60 min before incision; repeat if surgery > 4 hours
