## Overview

Methadone has unique pharmacokinetics — its half-life is long and variable (8-60+ hours), causing delayed accumulation. QT prolongation is a significant concern. It is also used in opioid use disorder treatment.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Methadone: unpredictable long half-life — peak respiratory depression can be DELAYED (days). Monitor QTc (Torsades risk). Multiple naloxone doses needed for reversal. Prescribed for OUD through licensed clinics only.
