## Overview

Dexamethasone is a potent, long-acting glucocorticoid with minimal mineralocorticoid activity. It is used for cerebral edema, croup, COVID-19, nausea prevention, and as a diagnostic agent in Cushing syndrome testing.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Dexamethasone: LEAST mineralocorticoid activity — less edema/K+ loss. Used for CEREBRAL EDEMA (vasogenic, not cytotoxic). Dexamethasone suppression test for Cushing. COVID-19 mortality reduction in severe cases.
