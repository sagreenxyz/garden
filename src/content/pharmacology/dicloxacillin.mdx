## Overview

Dicloxacillin is the oral antistaphylococcal penicillin for mild-moderate MSSA infections. Its absorption is reduced 50% by food, requiring administration on an empty stomach.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Dicloxacillin: take on EMPTY STOMACH (food reduces absorption 50%). Oral MSSA treatment. Does NOT cover MRSA. Complete full course.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Dicloxacillin = empty stomach'** — food reduces absorption by half
