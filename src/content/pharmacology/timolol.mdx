## Overview

Timolol is used both systemically and as an ophthalmic preparation for glaucoma. Even topical ophthalmic drops can cause systemic beta blockade, including bronchospasm.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Even OPHTHALMIC timolol can cause systemic beta blockade — bronchospasm in asthma patients. Apply nasolacrimal pressure after instillation to reduce absorption.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Eye drops = systemic effects'** — timolol eye drops can still cause bronchospasm
