## Overview

Losartan is the prototype ARB with additional evidence for reducing stroke risk in hypertension with LVH. It does not cause the bradykinin-mediated cough seen with ACE inhibitors.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ ARBs are preferred over ACE inhibitors when dry cough is intolerable. ARBs also cause angioedema (rarely). Avoid in pregnancy — same as ACE inhibitors. Monitor K+ and renal function.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'ARBs block the receptor, not the enzyme'** — no cough, same pregnancy risk as ACE inhibitors
