## Overview

Ciprofloxacin is the oral antibiotic with unique activity against Pseudomonas aeruginosa. It is the drug of choice for anthrax exposure. Black Box Warnings include tendon rupture, peripheral neuropathy, and myasthenia gravis worsening.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Ciprofloxacin Black Box Warnings: TENDON RUPTURE (Achilles), PERIPHERAL NEUROPATHY (irreversible), MYASTHENIA GRAVIS worsening. Chelation with dairy/antacids — separate by 2 hours. ANTHRAX drug of choice.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Fluoroquinolones = tendon rupture + neuropathy'** — stop immediately for tendon pain; separate from dairy/antacids
