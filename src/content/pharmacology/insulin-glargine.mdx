## Overview

Insulin glargine provides peakless 24-hour basal insulin coverage. It must NEVER be mixed with other insulins, as this alters its pH-dependent mechanism. It appears clear but is distinct from regular insulin.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Insulin glargine: NEVER MIX with other insulins (pH change destroys mechanism). Peakless 24-hour coverage. Clear but NOT regular insulin — label clearly. Give at same time daily.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Glargine = basal insulin, never mix'** — peakless 24-hour coverage; clear but cannot be mixed
