## Overview

Exenatide is a GLP-1 receptor agonist that lowers blood glucose in a glucose-dependent manner (less hypoglycemia risk) while promoting weight loss. Nausea is the most common initial side effect.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ , GLP-1 agonists: Black Box Warning for MEDULLARY THYROID CANCER — avoid in MTC/MEN2 history. Nausea is expected initially. Monitor for pancreatitis. Weight LOSS benefit. Byetta: give 60 min before meals.
