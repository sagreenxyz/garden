## Overview

Indapamide is a thiazide-like diuretic with direct vasodilatory properties and a more metabolically neutral profile than standard thiazides — less effect on glucose and lipids.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Indapamide: thiazide-like with vasodilation, more metabolically neutral. Monitor K+ and Na+. Works similarly to HCTZ.

## Drug Class Memory Aid

**'Indapamide = metabolically neutral thiazide'** — less effect on glucose/lipids than HCTZ
