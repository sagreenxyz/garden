## Overview

Micafungin is an echinocandin antifungal that targets the fungal cell wall rather than the membrane. It has excellent safety with minimal nephrotoxicity and drug interactions, making it well-tolerated for candida infections.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Micafungin: echinocandin — targets CELL WALL (glucan synthase inhibitor). Minimal nephrotoxicity (unlike amphotericin). Minimal drug interactions. Active against azole-resistant Candida. Monitor LFTs.
