## Overview

Diclofenac has a higher risk of hepatotoxicity compared to other NSAIDs, requiring liver function monitoring. Its topical formulation provides local anti-inflammatory effects with minimal systemic absorption.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Diclofenac: higher HEPATOTOXICITY than other NSAIDs — monitor LFTs. Topical form (Voltaren gel) has minimal systemic absorption. Avoid in severe liver disease.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
