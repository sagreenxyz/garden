## Overview

Cefoxitin is a cephamycin (2nd generation) with unique anaerobic coverage including Bacteroides fragilis, making it useful for intraabdominal, pelvic, and colorectal surgical prophylaxis.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Cefoxitin: ONLY cephalosporin with significant anaerobic coverage (Bacteroides fragilis). Used for intraabdominal infections and colorectal surgical prophylaxis.

## Drug Class Memory Aid
