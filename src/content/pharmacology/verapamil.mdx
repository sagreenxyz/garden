## Overview

Verapamil is the most cardiodepressant CCB, strongly suppressing AV node conduction. It is useful for SVT but dangerous in WPW syndrome where it can accelerate ventricular response via the accessory pathway.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Verapamil in WPW = DANGEROUS — can cause VF. Constipation is the most common side effect. Strongest negative inotrope of all CCBs — avoid in HF. Never IV verapamil + IV beta blocker.
