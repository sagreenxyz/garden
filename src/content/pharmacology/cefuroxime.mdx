## Overview

Cefuroxime is a 2nd-generation cephalosporin with improved gram-negative coverage (H. influenzae) compared to cefazolin. Oral Ceftin should be taken with food to improve absorption.

## NCLEX High-Yield Points

> ⚠️ Cefuroxime (2nd gen): better gram-negative coverage including H. influenzae. Used for early Lyme disease. Oral form should be taken WITH FOOD (improves absorption).
