## Welcome

This encyclopedia entry explains the foundational concepts of this LMS.

### Content Types

- **Encyclopedia** – Reference articles on specific topics
- **Courses** – Structured learning paths with ordered lessons
- **Notes** – Quick personal notes and references
- **Presentations** – Slide-based content
- **Quizzes** – Assessments to test knowledge
