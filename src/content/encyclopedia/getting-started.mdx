## Welcome

This encyclopedia entry explains the foundational concepts of this <KeyTerm entry={lms} client:load />.

### Content Types

- **Encyclopedia** – Authoritative reference articles on specific topics
- **Courses** – Structured learning paths with ordered lessons
- **Notes** – Quick personal notes and references
- **Presentations** – Web-based presentations built as `.astro` pages
- **Quizzes** – Assessments to test knowledge

### Technology

Content is authored in <KeyTerm entry={mdx} client:load /> files, which let you embed interactive
Svelte components directly in your writing. All content files are validated through

<KeyTerm entry={cc} client:load /> with strict Zod schemas.
