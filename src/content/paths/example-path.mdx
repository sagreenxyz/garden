## About This Path

The **NCLEX Prep Track** walks you through foundational nursing concepts in a carefully ordered sequence. Each course builds on the last, reinforcing clinical judgment at every step.

## What You'll Learn

- Core LMS navigation and study strategies
- Vital signs assessment and interpretation
- Infection prevention and standard precautions
- Patient communication using motivational interviewing techniques

Complete all courses in order and attempt the associated quizzes to benchmark your readiness.
