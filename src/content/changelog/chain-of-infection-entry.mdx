## What's new

The **Chain of Infection** encyclopedia entry explains the classic epidemiological
model used to understand and interrupt disease transmission in clinical settings.

### Topics covered

- Infectious agent, reservoir, portal of exit, mode of transmission, portal of
  entry, and susceptible host
- Evidence-based nursing interventions at each link
- Connections to the AACN Essentials (D3) and NCLEX-NG Safety category (SECE)
