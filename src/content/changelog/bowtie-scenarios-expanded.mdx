## What's new

The **NGN Bowtie Question Bank** has been expanded with eight new clinical judgment
scenarios. Each scenario presents a patient vignette and asks learners to identify the
correct condition, select two priority nursing actions, and choose two parameters to
monitor — mirroring the Next Generation NCLEX Bowtie item format.

### New scenarios added

- **Pulmonary Embolism** — post-operative hypoxia and hemodynamic compromise
- **Anaphylaxis** — medication error triggering a life-threatening systemic reaction
- **Acute Kidney Injury (AKI)** — aminoglycoside nephrotoxicity with oliguria and hyperkalemia
- **Hypertensive Crisis** — severe hypertension with end-organ damage and new neurological deficits
- **Acute Respiratory Distress Syndrome (ARDS)** — severe refractory hypoxemia requiring intubation
- **Increased Intracranial Pressure (ICP)** — Cushing triad and herniation signs after traumatic brain injury
- **Postpartum Hemorrhage** — uterine atony with bladder distension following augmented labor
- **Severe Hypoglycemia** — unconscious diabetic patient requiring IV dextrose

### Features

- Detailed clinical rationale for every correct and incorrect answer choice
- Progress tracking with best score and attempt count saved locally
- Alignment with NCLEX-NG Clinical Judgment Measurement Model (CJMM)
