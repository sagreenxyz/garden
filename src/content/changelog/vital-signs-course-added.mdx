## What's new

The **Vital Signs Assessment** course is now live. It walks learners through
each of the five core vital signs, explains normal adult ranges, and discusses
common abnormal findings and their clinical significance.

### Highlights

- Step-by-step measurement technique for each vital sign
- Interactive knowledge check at the end of each section
- Alignment with NCLEX-NG Client Needs (PhysI) and QSEN competencies (EBP, PCC)
